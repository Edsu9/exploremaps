Our Story

Passionate about travel, dedicated to excellence

Mabuhay! Explore Maps Travel and Tours aims to deliver exceptional service that meets the diverse needs of our clients, ensuring their complete satisfaction. Our team comprises seasoned professionals dedicated to providing unparalleled travel experiences. We believed that our clients deserve effortless, comfortable, and memorable adventures that make their trips and vacations truly unforgettable.

Explore Maps Travel and Tours offer a comprehensive range of travel services, including domestic and International flight bookings, as well as hotel accommodation tailored to your needs and interest. Our travel agency recognize the importance of travel documents to have a smooth journey, so we ensure that you're fully equipped with all the necessary paperwork such as visa processing, travel Insurance, and other necessary documents needed in your trip. Furthermore, we also offer tour packages locally and internationally, Airbnb, car rentals and 24/7 customer service. Driven by a passion for exploration and a proven track record of delivering outstanding travel experiences, Explore Maps Travel and Tours is your passport to adventure.
