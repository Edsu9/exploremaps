Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our accommodation services

What types of accommodation do you offer?

We offer a wide range of accommodation options including beach resorts, city hotels, Airbnb properties, and eco-lodges. Our selection caters to different preferences and budgets, ensuring that every traveler finds their perfect stay in Sorsogon.

How far in advance should I book my accommodation?

We recommend booking your accommodation at least 2-3 months in advance, especially if you're planning to visit during peak season (December to May) or during local festivals. For last-minute bookings, please contact us directly, and we'll do our best to find suitable options.

What payment methods do you accept for accommodation bookings?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, credit/debit cards, PayPal, and GCash. For most bookings, we require a deposit (typically 30-50% of the total amount) to secure your reservation, with the balance payable before or upon check-in, depending on the accommodation's policy.

Can you arrange special requests for my accommodation?

Yes, we can arrange special requests such as room decorations for special occasions, specific room locations, dietary requirements, airport transfers, and more. Please inform us of any special requests when making your booking, and we'll do our best to accommodate them.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary depending on the accommodation provider. Generally, cancellations made 30 days or more before check-in receive a full refund minus processing fees. Cancellations made 15-29 days before check-in typically receive a 50% refund. Cancellations made less than 15 days before check-in are usually non-refundable. We'll provide the specific cancellation policy for your booking during the reservation process.
