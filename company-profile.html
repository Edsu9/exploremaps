About Our Company

Professional travel services you can trust

Explore Maps Travel and Tours is headquartered in Unit 6, CLI Complex, Brgy. Cabid-an, Sorsogon City. Established on, our office is committed to providing exceptional travel services. We are a duly licensed travel agency, registered with the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) under certificate number 6893480. Furthermore, we are registered with the Bureau of Internal Revenue (BIR) under Tax Identification Number (TIN) 391-407-900-00000, demonstrating our compliance with all tax laws and regulations.

As a full-service travel agency, we take pride in our professional team of travel consultants who are dedicated to creating memorable experiences for our clients. Our expertise spans domestic and international destinations, with a special focus on showcasing the natural beauty and cultural heritage of Sorsogon province.

We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients through exceptional service, transparent business practices, and personalized attention to detail. Whether you're planning a family vacation, a corporate retreat, or a solo adventure, our team is here to make your travel dreams a reality.
